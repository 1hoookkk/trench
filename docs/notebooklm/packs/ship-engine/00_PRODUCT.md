# TRENCH — Product Identity

## One Sentence

Every cartridge is a body with a law, a behavior, and a way to break.

## Paradigm Shift

Instead of "What topology is this filter?" → **"What body does this impose, how does it resist, and where does it scar?"**

## What It Is

TRENCH is a finite canon of non-linear morphological resonators. Each cartridge is a self-contained physics engine governed by three behavioral laws, navigated via a terrain that fights back, powered by DSP imperfections weaponized into acoustic folklore.

The constraint is the product.

## Three Pillars

### 1. The 3-Layer Behavioral Stack

Every cartridge answers three questions:

| Layer | Question | Maps To |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Core** | What body has the signal entered? | The invariant identity that survives any input. |
| **Character** | How does that body move under normal use? | The everyday verb, the dialect. |
| **Extreme** | What happens when you break it? | The cult behavior, the failure mode that triggers addiction. |

This replaces "filter type" as an organizing principle. Naming emerges from the stack: *Pressure Vault = Containment / Ring / Buckle.*

### 2. The XY Pad as Physical Terrain

The morph surface is a rheological arena, not Cutoff x Resonance.

- **Basins** — Core zones. Smooth and gravitational. The body's invariant truth.
- **Mid-zones** — Character emerges through hysteresis and viscous drag.
- **Ridges / edges** — Extreme failure states that require deliberate motor-ritual to reach.

Difficulty is the point, not the problem. The puck has mass, momentum, and elastic tethering to the actual coefficient state.

### 3. Weaponized DSP Artifacts

Five mechanisms are named and mapped:

| Mechanism | Acoustic Result |
|-----------|----------------|
| Coefficient shot noise | Acoustic friction |
| DC-biased asymmetric saturation | Bodily wetness |
| Slew-rate limited Q recovery | Elastic squelch |
| Interpolation overshoot | Topological scars |
| Critical-band pole alignment | Auditory terror |

These are the material properties of the instrument, not bugs.

## What It Is Not

- Not a multi-filter utility.
- Not a preset browser.
- Not a heritage museum.
- Not a drum bus sweetener.
- Not a general-purpose insert.

## Library Organization

Cartridges are grouped by **House** — a behavioral family sharing spectral ancestry or movement character.

Within each House, cartridges occupy one of three **Profiles**: Core / Character / Extreme (see `01_HOUSES_AND_PROFILES.md`).

8–12 bodies ship. Every one must win on real material.

## Controls

Three controls: **MORPH** / **Q** / **TYPE**.
Plus ENV toggle (envelope follower).
No mod matrix. No routing. No menu diving.

## Success Condition

The system worked if users describe cartridges with gestures, metaphors, and names — not parameter values.

## Product Boundary

- **TRENCH** = VST3/CLAP plugin. Brushed metal chassis, CRT phosphor green display.
- **TRENCHWORK** = forge/workbench for generating, previewing, rating, and exporting cartridges into TRENCH.
- Do not blur scope, naming, or responsibilities.
