Three houses for body classification:

- **Core**: Utility, everyday, completely functional. Reliable workhorse filters.
- **Character**: Musical, expressive. Interesting tonal behavior worth exploring.
- **Extreme**: Wild, unstable, unpredictable. The character fractures or erupts.

**Why:** Houses define the behavioral family a body belongs to. They inform curation decisions, shipping tiers, and which bodies get promoted vs held.

**How to apply:** When classifying bodies, map behavioral data (Q persistence, morph coherence, stability, cliffs) to houses. Core = Strong/Strong + stable. Character = mixed persistence with musical movement. Extreme = Weak/Weak or high cliff count or talkingness that dies/erupts.
