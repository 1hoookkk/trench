"A shortcut to spectral taste you could not dial in manually."

**Why:** This is the user's definitive product positioning. Not "a filter plugin." Not "Z-Plane emulation." The value proposition is access to spectral character that manual EQ cannot reach.

**How to apply:** Every feature decision, UI label, and generation strategy should serve this. If a body can be replicated with a parametric EQ, it fails this test. The forge's job is to produce things that require 12-stage cascade interaction, zero-forcing, and betrayal to exist.
