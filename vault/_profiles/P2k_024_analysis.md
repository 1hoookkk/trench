## MegaSweepz (P2k_024, filterType=24)

### What It Is
A comb‑jaw “mega sweeper” that punches ~-300 dB voids and snaps between a sealed low‑mid mouth box and a bright speaking mask. At low Q it’s dark, hollow, and pressurized (sibilance is literally notched out); at high Q it rebuilds into a vented 1–3 kHz vocal faceplate. Use it for talking risers, chewing basses, and pads that need aggressive moving hollows rather than a polite sweep.

### Stage Roles
- Stage 1: bite/presence hinge at 1.40–4.04 kHz (presence peak ↔ upper edge). Both. Stress.
- Stage 2: throat formant rung at 0.55–1.10 kHz (F1 → low F2). Moves with Q. Identity.
- Stage 3: tongue carrier at 0.60–1.83 kHz (F1→F2 ladder). Both. Identity.
- Stage 4: bridge jaw at 0.38–3.09 kHz (throat floor ↔ 3 kHz mask node). Both. Stress.
- Stage 5: chest/room floor at 128–412 Hz (chest resonance ↔ upper throat). Both. Anchor.
- Stage 6: roof vent at 0.58–3.12 kHz, with a near-unit-circle notch that jumps ~6.05/7.58 kHz → 17.96 kHz. Both. Cavity.

### Motion Law
At Q0, Stages 2/3/4/5/6 bunch into a single mouth cavity (≈0.32–0.60 kHz) while Stage 1 sits up at ~4 kHz like a tooth. Morph *unfolds* it: Stage 4 climbs 376 → 1201 Hz, Stage 3 lifts 600 → 934 Hz, and Stage 1 drops 4035 → 1404 Hz, stretching the box into a 0.4–1.4 kHz vowel ladder.

Q is the real rebuild. `M0_Q0 -> M0_Q100`: the floor collapses to chest (Stage 5: 322 → 141 Hz) while Stages 4/1/6 lock into a ~3.1 kHz faceplate and the inner rungs land at ~1.1 kHz and ~1.83 kHz. Stage 6’s roof notch also ejects from ~6–7.6 kHz to ~18 kHz, turning “sealed” into vented air. This is the most violent corner jump: centroid 141 → 2640 Hz while peak/range drop (24.8 dB / 334.6 dB → 2.7 dB / 274.9 dB).

At high Q, morph barely moves frequencies; it tightens radii toward 0.999, making the same 1–3 kHz mask go from papery to razor (peak 2.7 → 17 dB; centroid → 6.1 kHz).

### What Would Break It
Stage 6. Without the near‑unit‑circle roof notch (and its Q‑driven relocation), you lose the sealed‑mouth → vented‑mask illusion and the “mega” hollowing; it devolves into a busy midrange formant stack.
