## 2 Pole Bandpass (P2k_006, filterType=6)

### What It Is
This is not acting like a plain 2-pole bandpass. It behaves like a mouth that can gather into a speaking midrange cavity or burst apart into a whistle crown with one low bone left underneath. The key state is M100_Q0: four stages pile into 1.1-2.0 kHz, one anchor stays at 159 Hz, and one lid hangs near 10 kHz, so the filter suddenly sounds embodied and vocal. At M100_Q100 that body is gone; nearly everything shoots into 8.6-14.7 kHz and peaks hard. A producer would use this to pivot a source between throaty speech pressure and cutting metallic glare.

### Stage Roles
- Stage 1: bridge from 1.2-2.3 kHz mouth to 8.6 kHz sibilant blade. Both. Stress.
- Stage 2: anchor at 79-364 Hz, from room/chest murmur to vocal-tract floor. Both. Anchor.
- Stage 3: formant at 479 Hz to 10.7 kHz, crossing from nasal throat into upper bite. Both. Identity.
- Stage 4: bite at 1.4-4.2 kHz or air at 11.5-13.2 kHz. Both. Stress.
- Stage 5: air bridge at 2.1-15.5 kHz, from vocal edge into top-band glare. Both. Bridge.
- Stage 6: air/cavity lid at 8.1-17.4 kHz with a fixed 3.8-6.4 kHz zero underneath. Both. Cavity.

### Motion Law
Morph and Q trade jobs depending on where you start. At Q0, morph closes the filter into a body: Stages 1, 3, 4, and 5 collapse into a tight 1.2-2.0 kHz mouth, while Stage 2 stays low and Stage 6 remains the high lid. At M0, raising Q does a gentler opening move: the 79 Hz, 479 Hz, 13-17 kHz split reorganizes into a staircase at 257 Hz, 1.36 kHz, 2.3 kHz, 4.2 kHz, 6.4 kHz, and 8.1 kHz, so the shell becomes more intelligible without losing its roof. At M100, Q is violent instead. It tears the mouth back apart: Stages 1 and 3-6 jump out of 1.2-10 kHz into an 8.6-14.7 kHz whistle crown, leaving Stage 2 alone at 364 Hz. The harshest transition is M100_Q0 to M100_Q100, where a full throat-and-mouth body gets stripped to one low bone plus a metallic halo. It feels physical because one stage keeps the body weight while the others either gather into a jaw or peel off into teeth and air.

### What Would Break It
If Stage 2 weakened, the whole filter would lose its body reference. The other stages can gather into a mouth or explode into a whistle, but Stage 2 keeps those changes attached to a chest floor. Without it, the filter becomes mid-high combing with no bone underneath.

### Relatives
Closest to P2k_005 in the way low-Q morph can build a mouth under a high lid, but P2k_006 is less stable because high-Q morph destroys that body. P2k_004 is a harsher cousin with similar shell-versus-body conflict. P2k_002 is the opposite temperament: its Q move rebuilds throat more consistently, while P2k_006 can suddenly abandon it.
