## Contrary Bandpass (P2k_008, filterType=8)

### What It Is
This is a pressure mask that only reveals its mouth when Q rises. At low Q, most of the structure lives in the air band and upper bite, so the sound reads like a bright shell with a hidden throat. Push Q and the shell folds downward into a speaking ladder around 500 Hz to 4.4 kHz, turning metallic glare into a tight, nasal bark. A producer would reach for this when they want a source to sound forced through a mouthpiece: from brittle top-end skin into articulated midrange speech pressure.

### Stage Roles
- Stage 1: air lid at 3.6-4.4 kHz under low-Q ultrasonic glare. Moves with Q. Stress.
- Stage 2: bite/formant carrier at 849 Hz to 2.8 kHz. Both. Identity.
- Stage 3: throat seed at 541-740 Hz; effectively absent at M0_Q0, then becomes load-bearing. Both. Identity.
- Stage 4: presence bridge at 3.6-4.4 kHz, or hidden air shell at 14.5-15.6 kHz. Moves with Q. Bridge.
- Stage 5: bridge from 2.15-2.5 kHz mouth bite to 10.4-11.0 kHz shell. Moves with Q. Cavity.
- Stage 6: anchor that flips between 166-587 Hz throat floor and 2.8-11.2 kHz roof. Both. Anchor.

### Motion Law
Q does the big anatomical move. At both morph extremes, raising Q drags nearly the whole filter out of the air band and down into a stacked speech body: Stage 1 drops from 12-14 kHz to about 4 kHz, Stage 4 falls from 14-15 kHz to 3.6-4.4 kHz, and Stages 2, 3, 5, and 6 gather between 500 Hz and 2.8 kHz. That is why the dynamic range jumps from about 66 dB to 100-124 dB and the centroid falls by more than 1.1 kHz. Morph is quieter at low Q, but at high Q it becomes the real contrary mechanism: Stage 2 climbs from 849 Hz to 2.8 kHz while Stage 6 drops from 2.84 kHz to 587 Hz, so the body stretches in opposite directions instead of sweeping as one block. The most violent transition is M100_Q0 to M100_Q100, where a mostly invisible shell suddenly acquires a hard throat floor and a spoken upper mouth. It feels physical because the filter does not just brighten or darken; it folds its roof into a face.

### What Would Break It
If Stage 6 weakened, the filter would lose the hinge that makes the anatomy believable. It is the stage that either supplies the hidden roof at low Q or the throat floor at high Q. Without that inversion point, the shell-to-mouth transformation collapses into ordinary upper-mid filtering.

### Relatives
Closest to P2k_006 and P2k_007 because all three can build a mouth out of a brighter shell, but P2k_008 is more organized: Q performs the main downward fold, then morph counter-rotates the inner stages instead of simply pushing everything upward. Opposite of P2k_000, whose anatomy stays more combed and fractured even when it grows a throat.
