## Swept EQ 2/1 Octave (P2k_010, filterType=10)

### What It Is
This is not a normal swept bell. It behaves like a rigid mouth mask bolted onto a low throat, then pushed upward until the mask turns into sibilant metal. At low Q it speaks with a dense, forward midrange body around 0.5-4.4 kHz; at high Q the upper half peels off into presence, sibilance, and air while one low stage stays behind as the last bit of chest. A producer would use it to force a sound from nasal speech pressure into a hard bright visor without losing all bodily reference.

### Stage Roles
- Stage 1: formant carrier from 849 Hz throat-mouth to 1.79 kHz upper-mouth. Moves with Q. Identity.
- Stage 2: low anchor from 225-550 Hz chest/AE-EH floor against a 1.4-1.7 kHz cavity zero. Both. Anchor.
- Stage 3: bridge from 2.15-2.94 kHz ear-canal bite. Moves with both. Bridge.
- Stage 4: bite ridge from 2.83-4.85 kHz, crossing from singer's ring into sharp presence. Moves with Q. Stress.
- Stage 5: presence/sibilance shell from 3.61 kHz to 8.20 kHz. Both. Air.
- Stage 6: upper lid from 4.35 kHz presence to 8.85 kHz sibilant roof. Both. Cavity.

### Motion Law
Morph barely relocates the anatomy at low Q. It mostly tightens the same six-stage scaffold: the pole frequencies stay nearly fixed while radii harden, so the mouth becomes more pressurized without changing shape. Q is the real reconfiguration. Stage 1 jumps upward from 849 Hz to 1.79 kHz while Stage 2 drops from 541-550 Hz to about 225 Hz, so the body splits into a low chest remnant and a lifted upper mouth instead of sweeping as one block. At the same time Stages 4, 5, and 6 all climb together from 2.8/3.6/4.4 kHz into 4.9/8.2/8.8 kHz, turning presence into a bright roof. The most violent transition is `M100_Q0 -> M100_Q100`: dynamic range drops nearly 28 dB while the centroid rises by about 1 kHz, which means the dense vocal mask thins out and stretches into a sharper shell. It feels physical because one bone stays low while the rest of the face lifts away from it.

### What Would Break It
Stage 2 is load-bearing. Remove that 225-550 Hz floor and the filter loses the body that makes the upper migration feel like a face opening upward. Without it, this collapses into a bright stacked EQ with no throat under the metal.

### Relatives
Closest to P2k_009 because both are misnamed swept-EQ shells that keep a bodily low stage while the upper structure detaches into metal. P2k_010 is more disciplined: Q lifts three upper stages as a linked visor while P2k_009 cross-swaps its body more violently. Opposite of P2k_008, whose main move is a roof folding down into speech; this one pushes speech upward until it becomes roof.
