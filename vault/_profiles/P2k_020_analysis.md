## Dual EQ Morph/Expression (P2k_020, filterType=20)

### What It Is
This is a fixed “talking mask” scaffold you can tense and un-tense, not a sweeping dual EQ. At low Q it feels like a bright plate with a carved presence scar: chest is barely there, the roof lives in sibilance, and a notch cuts a hole around 4–5 kHz. Push Q and the whole roof folds down into the speech band, assembling a stacked vowel ladder (≈540 Hz, 850 Hz, 2.1–2.9 kHz, 3.6–4.4 kHz) while the air gets guillotined (notches reach ~-100 dB). Use it to make a synth/bass “say words”, clamp drums into a boxed speaker-mask, or turn pads into a pressurized mouthpiece.

### Stage Roles
- Stage 1: bite/air lid at 3.6–8.2 kHz. Moves with Q. Stress.
- Stage 2: anchor at 222–541 Hz. Moves with Q. Anchor.
- Stage 3: formant carrier at 0.85–1.79 kHz. Moves with Q. Identity.
- Stage 4: teeth plate at 4.35–8.85 kHz. Moves with Q. Stress.
- Stage 5: formant mask at 2.14–2.90 kHz. Moves with Q. Identity.
- Stage 6: cavity scar at ~3.95–4.78 kHz (low Q) or an air-cut at ~18 kHz (high Q). Both. Cavity.

### Motion Law
Everything is a collapse choreography, not six independent sweeps. With Q low, Stage 1 + Stage 4 live up in the sibilance roof (8–9 kHz) while the “mouth” is split between Stage 3 (~1.8 kHz) and a mask node near 2.9 kHz, and Stage 6 carves a movable hole in the presence band. Raising Q drags the roof down together into 3.6–4.4 kHz, pulls the mask toward 2.1–2.8 kHz, and snaps the low anchor up into a real F1 (~540 Hz) while Stage 3 drops into an F2-ish 850 Hz hinge — anchor rises as the mouth drops. The violent move is the Q transition: dynamic range almost doubles and the filter starts behaving like a tube with sections of near-silence between ribs. Morph mostly doesn’t move the bones — it changes stiffness: poles across the cascade tighten, making the same anatomy feel more clenched and “spoken”, and at low Q it slides the Stage 6 scar from ~3.95 kHz to ~4.78 kHz like changing where the teeth scrape.

### What Would Break It
Stage 6. Without that near-unit-circle zero, the filter loses the “expression” carve (and the ~-100 dB voids at high Q) and reads like a busy resonant EQ instead of a physical mouthpiece.

### Relatives
