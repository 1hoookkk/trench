## Bat Phaser (P2k_014, filterType=14)

### What It Is
This is not a batty swirl and barely a phaser in the usual sense. It starts as a body: chest at 90-357 Hz, throat around 1 kHz, then a mouth and bite ladder through 1.7-2.5 kHz. Push morph and Q together and that body explodes upward into a hard crown around 8-14 kHz, with only a narrow 1.4 kHz spine left underneath. A producer would reach for it when a sound needs to feel like pressure moving from rib cage to cracked metal visor: from voiced cavity to airborne fracture.

### Stage Roles
- Stage 1: anchor that flips from 90 Hz chest floor to 5.1-12.6 kHz air lid. Both. Anchor.
- Stage 2: formant spine at 357 Hz to 1.44 kHz. Moves with Q. Identity.
- Stage 3: bridge from 1.0-2.3 kHz throat/upper mouth to 10.0 kHz shard band. Both. Bridge.
- Stage 4: bite carrier at 1.75-4.14 kHz, then 11.2 kHz blade. Both. Stress.
- Stage 5: presence roof at 2.5-4.14 kHz, then 8.4 kHz air plate. Both. Stress.
- Stage 6: anchor/cavity hinge from 456-645 Hz throat floor to 14.5-16.7 kHz brittle roof. Both. Anchor.

### Motion Law
The core law is a cross-swap, not a sweep. At `M0_Q0`, Stages 1-5 build a believable torso-to-mouth ladder while Stage 6 hangs as a brittle roof. Raising either morph or Q to a single extreme already flips Stage 1 out of the chest and drags Stage 6 down into the throat, so the filter inverts its top and bottom supports. The violent move happens only at `M100_Q100`: Stage 6 snaps back up to 14.5 kHz, Stage 3 jumps from the 2.2 kHz speech zone to 10 kHz, and Stages 4-5 leave the presence band for an 8-11 kHz blade cluster. That is why peak level rises by about 24 dB across Q at high morph while the diagonal motion loses over 100 dB of notch depth. Morph opens the skull; Q hardens the edges. It feels physical because the chest, throat, and roof do not glide together: they trade jobs like a jaw dislocating and reforming higher in the head.

### What Would Break It
Stage 6 is the load-bearing hinge. Without it, the filter loses the low-throat support at the middle corners and the final high-roof snap at `M100_Q100`. Then the whole identity collapses into an ordinary upper-mid ladder instead of a body that can invert into shards.
